All About Animals

An encyclopedia is a comprehensive exploration of the rich and intriguing world of animals, with special attention given to endangered and vulnerable species. I like to share my knowledge with others, and I would like people to know more about animals. I find animals extremely fascinating, and I am always wanting to know more so I figured that other people like me could visit this site to learn something new or exciting about animals. On this website I categorized animals into two categories exotic and domestic. An "exotic animal" typically refers to any non-native animal species, or sometimes even rare or unusual species. A domestic animal is a species that has been selectively bred to fit what we wanted/needed.

Why animals are so important!

Animals and insects are the only reason plants and ecosystems work. A lot of ecosystems are at risk of collapsing because of one or more animals being endangered or near extinction. If more people learn about animals and why we need them to survive then maybe we can save some of these ecosystems on the edge of destruction.
