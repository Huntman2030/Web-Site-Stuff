Frequently Asked Questions

What is a domesticated animal?
An animal that is not wild and is kept as a pet or to produce food.
What is an exotic animal?
An animal that is not domesticated and lives in the wild far away from where you live.
What is the difference of a domesticated animal and an exotic animal?
Exotic often refers to a species which is not native or indigenous to your location while domesticated is a local animal that was bred to be more friendly and less hostile to humans.
